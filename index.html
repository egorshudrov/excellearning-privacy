Privacy Policy for Excel Learning App

Last updated: November 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Excel Learning App ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address: Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Display name: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and profile picture

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Learning progress: Quiz scores, completed lessons, and learning statistics
  • App performance data: Crash reports and diagnostic information to improve the app

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Excel Learning App service
  • Authenticate your account and enable sign-in
  • Track your learning progress and personalize your experience
  • Send important updates about the app (if you opt in)
  • Improve app functionality and fix bugs

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

  • Authentication data: Managed by Firebase Authentication
  • Progress data: Stored in Firebase Firestore database
  • Security: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Location: Data is stored on Firebase servers (primarily in the United States)

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We only share data with:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and data storage (required for app functionality)
  • Google Play Services: For Google Sign-In functionality

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Firestore: Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Delete your data: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correct your data: Update incorrect information in your profile
  • Export your data: Download your learning progress data

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts inactive for 3+ years may be automatically deleted

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
